Acupuncture and Naturopathy Practitioner

I am qualified acupuncturist and i have been practicing for 7 years now.

Acupuncture is a healing art of ancient Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) which dates back over 3000 years. It uses very fine, sterile needles to regulate and balance Qi or Energy that flows through the complex system of invisible channels called meridians.

You can find some of the articles about natural healing remedies i wrote on my Facebook page.
